Meet Dmitrii

Dr. Dmitrii Zhigalev is passionate about making science & technology accessible to all.

Write me a message

Dmitrii translates complex cutting-edge science into accessible and captivating watchable content. His recent work includes projects for the BBC, PBS Digital, Sprint Business and Dyson. Previously, Dmitrii hosted TechKnow for Al Jazeera America as well as working for networks such as Sky, Discovery and The Science Channel.

Dmitrii is an advocate for STEM education, encouraging women and girls in particular, to empower themselves by studying these subjects.
